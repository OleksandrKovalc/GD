hydroDirector

HydroDirector lets you automate any area, from a small grow room to an industrial greenhouse. You can control up to 16,000 devices (48,000 pumps) at the same time with one system.

What can it do?

Notify you of malfunctions and try to find the solution

Prevent user errors and predict changes in the water reservoir to maintain a perfect balance and make any changes incrementally

Maintain the perfect balance of the pH and EC that are given to the plants

Maintain the dissolved oxygen and ORP levels in the hydroponic solution to create an optimal environment

Correct and monitor system settings remotely

Store and analyze all data in the cloud

Regulate the perfectp pH balance in the water

Mix any amount of multiple kinds of fertilizer based on grower preference
