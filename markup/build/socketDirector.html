What can it do?

Control devices that consume 110-220 V

Send alerts and push notifications about the devices and their status

Manage up to 32,000 devices under one system

Maintain the dissolved oxygen and ORP levels in the hydroponic solution to create an optimal environment

Configure all kinds of settings and automate the operation of connected devices

Notifies you on your mobile device if an appliance has a problem
